    U.S.E. Finance Data Hub

    This site is maintained by the Finance section of Utrecht University School of Economics (U.S.E.) and is primarily intended for UU students and researchers. It provides documentation and manuals for accessing and working with financial data.

    UU students and staff: if you need help with data access or have questions about available resources, contact financedata.use@uu.nl
