# FactSet

## How to Get a FactSet Account

### Step 1: Register as a Student

Go to the <a href="https://support.factset.com/academic/#/student" target="_blank">FactSet Academic Student Registration</a> page and fill in the form:

- **Email**: Use your **@students.uu.nl** email address
- **University Name**: Universiteit Utrecht
- **Academic Degree**: Select the appropriate option
- **Expected Graduation Date**: Use **08/YYYY** (August of the year you graduate), e.g. 08/2026

### Step 2: Receive Your Credentials

You will receive your FactSet user ID and password by email. Check your spam folder if you do not see it.

### Step 3: Access FactSet

There are two options to use FactSet:

**Option 1: Solis Workspace (remote desktop)**

FactSet is installed on <a href="https://solisworkspace.uu.nl/Citrix/StoreWeb/" target="_blank">Solis Workspace</a>. After activating your FactSet ID, open FactSet there and enter your credentials.

**Option 2: Download FactSet on your own computer**

Download FactSet from <a href="https://www.factset.com/download" target="_blank">https://www.factset.com/download</a> and choose **General Release**.

- Includes a very useful **Excel Plugin**
- Not available for Apple (sorry)

### Learning How to Use FactSet

Our colleague Jeroen Derwall has recorded a series of short clips on how to use FactSet: <a href="https://www.jeroenderwall.nl/clips" target="_blank">https://www.jeroenderwall.nl/clips</a>

### Important Notes

- Even after receiving a confirmation email, FactSet might need some time to give full access rights (verification of the UU license).
